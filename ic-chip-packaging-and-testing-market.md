<p><strong>The IC Chip Packaging and Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BGA</li><li>LGA</li><li>SiP</li><li>FC</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC chip packaging and testing involves various types designed to protect and connect integrated circuits. Ball Grid Array (BGA) uses spherical balls for soldering, optimizing space and thermal performance. Land Grid Array (LGA) features flat pads for secure connections. System in Package (SiP) integrates multiple components into a single package, enhancing functionality. Flip Chip (FC) technology flips the chip for direct connection to substrates, improving electrical performance. Other packaging types include QFN, TQFP, and more, catering to diverse applications and performance needs.</p></p>
