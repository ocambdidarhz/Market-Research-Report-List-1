<p><p>Thermal Energy Storage Systems encompass various technologies to store and manage thermal energy. Solar Energy Storage captures excess solar heat for later use. Molten-Salt Technology stores heat in molten salts, providing consistent energy output. Hot Silicon Technology utilizes heated silicon for efficient thermal storage. Pumped-Heat Electricity Storage converts electricity to thermal energy, enabling energy dispatch during peak demand. Cryogenic Energy Storage employs low-temperature fluids to store and release energy, enhancing grid stability and supporting renewable integration.</p></p>
