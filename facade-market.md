<p><strong>The Facade Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-Ventilated Façade</li><li>Ventilated Façade</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Facade market types are categorized into non-ventilated facades, ventilated facades, and others. Non-ventilated facades involve cladding materials directly attached to the building structure, providing insulation and aesthetic appeal but limited airflow. Ventilated facades, on the other hand, feature a gap between the cladding and the insulation layer, allowing airflow that enhances thermal performance and moisture control. Other facade types may include structural glass facades, green walls, or composite systems, catering to varied architectural styles and functional requirements.</p></p>
