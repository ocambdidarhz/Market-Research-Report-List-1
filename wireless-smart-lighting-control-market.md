<p><p>The wireless smart lighting control market encompasses various communication technologies, each with unique features. Zigbee offers low power consumption and mesh networking, making it efficient for large setups. Wi-Fi provides high data rates and integrates easily with existing networks. Bluetooth is user-friendly for short-range applications but has limited range. Z-Wave focuses on home automation, offering robust security and low latency. EnOcean leverages energy-harvesting technologies, enabling self-powered devices, ideal for sustainable lighting solutions in smart homes and buildings.</p></p>
