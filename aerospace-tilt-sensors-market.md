<p><ul><li>Analog</li><li>Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace tilt sensors market is segmented into two primary types: analog and digital. Analog tilt sensors provide continuous output proportional to the angle of inclination, enabling real-time monitoring. They are often simpler and cost-effective for less complex applications. On the other hand, digital tilt sensors convert the angle data into a digital format, allowing for precise measurements and easier integration with modern avionics systems. Both types play critical roles in navigation, stability control, and safety systems within aerospace applications.</p></p>
