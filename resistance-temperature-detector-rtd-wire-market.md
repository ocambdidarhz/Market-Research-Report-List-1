<p><p>The Resistance Temperature Detector (RTD) wire market primarily features two types: thin-film and wire-wound RTDs. Thin-film RTDs are constructed using a thin layer of conductive material deposited on a substrate, offering rapid response times and stability. In contrast, wire-wound RTDs consist of a fine wire coiled around a ceramic or glass core, providing high accuracy and durability, ideal for extreme temperature applications. Both types serve various industries, ensuring precise temperature measurements in diverse environments.</p></p>
