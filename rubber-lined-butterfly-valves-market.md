<p><p>Rubber-lined butterfly valves are essential components in fluid control systems, designed to regulate flow in various industries. The two main market types are wafer type and lugged type. Wafer type valves are thin and lightweight, designed to fit between two flanges and are ideal for tight spaces. In contrast, lugged type valves feature threaded lugs that allow for easy installation and removal without needing to disturb the piping. Both types ensure effective sealing and durability, catering to different installation requirements.</p></p>
