<p><ul><li>Single-mode Switches</li><li>Multimode Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS-based switches are categorized into single-mode and multimode switches. Single-mode switches operate on a single communication channel, ensuring high precision and low power consumption, ideal for applications like optical networks. In contrast, multimode switches can manage multiple channels simultaneously, enhancing flexibility and functionality in complex systems. This adaptability makes multimode switches suitable for various applications, including telecommunications and data centers, where multiple connections are essential for efficient data transmission and resource management.</p></p>
