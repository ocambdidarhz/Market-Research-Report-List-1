<p><p>The drivetrain market is categorized into three main types: Front-Wheel Drive (FWD), Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD), and All-Wheel Drive (AWD). FWD vehicles channel power to the front wheels, enhancing fuel efficiency and traction on slippery surfaces, making them popular for compact cars. RWD offers a balanced weight distribution, ideal for performance and towing in trucks and sports cars. AWD provides power to all wheels, improving stability and traction, especially in varying weather conditions, making it a favored choice for SUVs and crossover vehicles.</p></p>
