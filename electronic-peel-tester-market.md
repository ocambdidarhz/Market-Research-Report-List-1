<p><p>The electronic peel tester market comprises several types based on the mechanism used for testing. Motor-based testers offer precise control for a variety of peel strength assessments. Hydraulic testers utilize fluid power for high-load applications, delivering consistent results even under intense strain. Pneumatic testers rely on compressed air, providing quick and efficient testing suitable for lighter materials.
