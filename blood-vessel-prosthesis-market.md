<p><strong>The Blood Vessel Prosthesis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Endovascular Stent Graft</li><li>Peripheral Vascular Graft</li><li>Bypass Graft</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The blood vessel prosthesis market comprises various types designed to restore vascular function. Endovascular stent grafts are minimally invasive devices used to treat aneurysms by reinforcing vessel walls. Peripheral vascular grafts replace or bypass blocked arteries in limbs, improving blood flow. Bypass grafts redirect blood around obstructions in the coronary or peripheral systems, enhancing circulation. Other prosthesis options may include patches and balloon-expandable stents, catering to diverse vascular treatment needs and advancing surgical outcomes.</p></p>
