<p><p>Whole Body Cryotherapy encompasses three primary market types: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Therapy. Liquid Nitrogen Therapy utilizes liquid nitrogen to rapidly cool the body, promoting recovery and reducing inflammation. Dry Ice Therapy employs solid carbon dioxide to achieve cold temperatures, offering similar benefits with a different methodology. Electric Therapy, on the other hand, utilizes specially designed machines to produce cold air, providing a controlled and consistent environment for cryotherapy. Each type serves various therapeutic and recovery purposes.</p></p>
