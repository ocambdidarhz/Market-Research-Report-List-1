HPTLC offers enhanced resolution and faster analysis, making it suitable for qualitative and quantitative assessments in pharmaceuticals and environmental testing. In contrast, Preparative PLC focuses on the separation of larger quantities of compounds for further analysis or application, such as in drug development and synthesis. Both types play vital roles in various scientific research fields.</p></p>
