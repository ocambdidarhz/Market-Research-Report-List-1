<p><strong>The Diaphragm Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weir diaphragm valves</li><li>Straightway diaphragm valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Diaphragm valves serve critical roles in fluid control across various industries. Weir diaphragm valves feature a raised seat design that minimizes pressure loss, making them suitable for applications involving slurries or viscous fluids. They offer excellent seal integrity and are commonly used in food processing and chemical industries. Straightway diaphragm valves, with a more linear flow pattern, facilitate straightforward flow control, ensuring minimal turbulence. These valves excel in applications where a smooth and efficient flow path is essential, such as in water treatment and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
