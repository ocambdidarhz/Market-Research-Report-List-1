<p><p>The Bio-BDO market involves two primary types: direct fermentation and biosuccinic acid. Direct fermentation utilizes microorganisms to convert biomass into butanediol (BDO) directly, offering a sustainable and efficient production method. In contrast, the biosuccinic acid market focuses on producing succinic acid through microbial fermentation, which can then be further processed into BDO. Both approaches prioritize renewable resources and environmentally friendly practices, making them increasingly relevant in the growing bioplastics and biofuels industries, promoting sustainable development.</p></p>
