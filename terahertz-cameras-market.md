<p><p>The terahertz cameras market is divided into two primary types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging detects naturally emitted terahertz radiation from objects, allowing for non-invasive inspection and analysis, particularly useful in safety and security applications. In contrast, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate objects, enabling detailed imaging and measurement, often applied in industrial and medical fields. Both types cater to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>
