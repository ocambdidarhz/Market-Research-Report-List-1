<p><p>Transparent vapor deposition packaging films, primarily SiOx (silicon oxide) and AlOx (aluminum oxide), are advanced materials used to enhance the barrier properties of packaging. SiOx offers excellent moisture and gas barrier capabilities while maintaining transparency, making it suitable for food and pharmaceutical products. In contrast, AlOx provides superior oxygen barrier properties and thermal stability. Both types ensure product longevity and protection against external elements, thus playing crucial roles in various industries, including food, electronics, and cosmetics.</p></p>
