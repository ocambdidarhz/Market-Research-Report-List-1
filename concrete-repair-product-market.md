<p><ul><li>One-Component Mortars</li><li>Two-Component Mortars</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The concrete repair product market is primarily divided into one-component and two-component mortars. One-component mortars are pre-mixed, requiring only the addition of water for application, making them user-friendly and suitable for small repairs. In contrast, two-component mortars consist of a resin and hardener that must be mixed prior to use, offering superior bonding and durability for more extensive repairs. Both types serve specific needs in construction, addressing various repair challenges while ensuring structural integrity and longevity.</p></p>
