<p><strong>The Medical & Hygiene PP Non Woven Fabric Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drylaid</li><li>Airlaid</li><li>Wetlaid</li><li>Spunlaid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical & Hygiene PP Non Woven Fabric market comprises four main types: Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid. Drylaid processes fibers that are air-laid and bonded through heat or adhesive, ideal for products like surgical gowns. Airlaid technology uses air to distribute fibers, creating absorbent materials for hygiene products. Wetlaid involves dispersing fibers in water to form a mat, suitable for medical applications. Spunlaid technologies, where melted polymers are extruded and laid down, produce strong, versatile fabrics for various medical uses.</p></p>
