<p><p>The bondable coating market consists of various solvents including methanol, acetone, methyl alcohol, and methyl ethyl ketone (MEK). Methyl alcohol, often interchangeable with methanol, serves similar purposes. MEK, with its ability to dissolve a wide range of polymers, effectively improves bonding and coating durability. Together, these solvents play crucial roles in enhancing various bondable coatings.</p></p>
