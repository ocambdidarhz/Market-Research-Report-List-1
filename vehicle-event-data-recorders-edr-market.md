<p><p>Vehicle Event Data Recorders (EDRs) are devices that capture data related to vehicle performance and driver behavior during critical events, such as accidents. The market for EDRs can be divided into two main types: independent and integrated. Independent EDRs are standalone devices that can be installed in any vehicle, providing flexibility for users.
